ToonAging: Face Re-Aging upon Artistic Portrait Style Transfer

Venue
CVPR AI4CC Workshop 2024
Authors
B. Kim, A. Muqeet, K. Lee, S. Seo
Keywords
Face Re-aging Style Transfer Diffusion Toon
Problem
Existing re-aging methods fail on artistic / cartoon-style portraits — they assume photorealistic inputs.
Method
Decouples style transfer and age editing: first transfer portrait style, then apply diffusion-based re-aging conditioned on the artistic domain.
Key Contributions
  • Domain-aware face re-aging pipeline for artistic portraits
  • Disentanglement of style and age attributes in latent space
  • Works across toon, painting, and sketch styles
Application
Film VFX, animation studios, entertainment content creation.

Minecraft-ify: Minecraft Style Image Generation with Text-guided Image Editing

Venue
NeurIPS ML4CD Workshop 2023 — Oral (Top 8.7%)
Authors
B. Kim, S. Byun, Y. Jung, W. Shin, S. Amin, S. Seo
Keywords
Text-guided Style Transfer Gaming In-game AI
Problem
Generating in-game assets in a consistent voxel/Minecraft aesthetic from arbitrary text prompts or reference images.
Method
Text-guided image editing pipeline fine-tuned on Minecraft asset distribution. Leverages IP-Adapter and domain-specific LoRA for stylistic consistency.
Key Contributions
  • First text-driven Minecraft-style generation system
  • Custom dataset of Minecraft asset–natural image pairs
  • Deployable in-game as real-time asset generator
Application
Procedural game content creation, rapid asset prototyping for game developers.

Explicitly Color-Inspired Neural Style Transfer Using Patchified AdaIN

Venue
CMES 2024 — Q2 Journal
Authors
B. Kim, W. Shin, Y. Jung, Y. Park, S. Seo
Keywords
Neural Style Transfer AdaIN Color Control
Problem
Standard AdaIN-based style transfer lacks explicit color control, leading to undesired hue shifts.
Method
Patch-level AdaIN normalization with a color-histogram guidance loss. Separates color and texture channels for independent control.
Key Contributions
  • Explicit color palette control in neural style transfer
  • Patchified AdaIN preserving local texture fidelity
  • User-specified color extraction from reference images

VideoTimeTravel: High-Fidelity Face Re-Aging Diffusion Models for Production Video

Venue
The Visual Computer 2026 — IF 2.9
Authors
B. Kim, Y. Nam, S. Seo
Keywords
Face Re-aging Video Diffusion Temporal Consistency
Problem
Frame-by-frame re-aging breaks temporal consistency in video — flickering across frames is a core bottleneck for production use.
Method
Diffusion-based video re-aging with temporal attention and cross-frame guidance, maintaining identity and age consistency across long sequences.

  • Cross-frame identity preservation mechanism
  • Evaluated on film-grade footage benchmarks
Application
Film post-production, digital de-aging for actors, streaming content.

Make VLM Recognize Visual Hallucination on Cartoon Character Image with Pose Information

Venue
WACV 2025
Authors
B. Kim*, W. Shin*, K. Lee, Y. Jung, S. Seo
Keywords
VLM Hallucination Cartoon Pose
Problem
VLMs frequently hallucinate character identities when posed cartoon images differ from their training distribution.
Method
Pose-conditioned visual prompting: inject skeleton pose information as an auxiliary visual signal to ground the VLM's character recognition.
Key Contributions
  • PA-ICVL dataset: pose-augmented cartoon visual language benchmark
  • Pose injection reduces hallucination rate significantly
  • ChatGPT-compatible demo deployed publicly

Video Face Re-Aging: Toward Temporally Consistent Face Re-Aging

Venue
arXiv 2023
Authors
A. Muqeet*, K. Lee*, B. Kim, Y. Hong, H. Lee, W. Kim, K. Lee
Keywords
Video Re-aging Temporal Face
Problem
Per-frame re-aging ignores video coherence — faces flicker and identity drifts across clips.
Method
Optical-flow guided temporal consistency module combined with a GAN-based re-aging backbone. Maintains identity across frames without per-clip finetuning.
Key Contributions
  • Temporal warp loss for frame-to-frame consistency
  • No per-video finetuning required
  • Real-time capable on consumer GPUs

Sounds That Shape: Audio-Driven 3D Mesh Generation with Attribute-Decoupled SDS

Venue
ICASSP 2026
Authors
B. Kim, S. Seo
Keywords
Audio-to-3D SDS Mesh Generation Multimodal
Problem
Generating 3D geometry from audio cues is underexplored — most 3D generation relies on text or image prompts alone.
Method
Attribute-decoupled Score Distillation Sampling (SDS): separates material and geometry guidance signals, conditioning shape generation on audio embeddings from a pretrained audio encoder.
Key Contributions
  • First audio-driven 3D mesh generation pipeline
  • Attribute-decoupled SDS: separates shape vs. material
  • Audio → geometry correspondence without paired data
Application
Procedural audio-visual game assets, immersive XR environments, creative tools.

Taming Gaussian Densification in Score Distillation Sampling for High-Fidelity Text-to-3D

Venue
CGI 2026 — Under Review
Authors
B. Kim, J. Ryu, S. Seo
Keywords
3DGS SDS Text-to-3D Densification
Problem
3D Gaussian Splatting combined with SDS suffers from uncontrolled Gaussian densification — leading to floaters, artifacts, and geometry collapse.
Method
Adaptive densification controller that monitors SDS gradient statistics and prunes/splits Gaussians based on geometric confidence scores during optimization.
Key Contributions
  • Principled densification schedule guided by SDS signal quality
  • Significant reduction in floaters and surface artifacts
  • Compatible with existing text-to-3D SDS pipelines

MultiFloodSynth: Multi-Annotated Flood Synthetic Dataset Generation

Venue
AAAI GoodData Workshop 2025 — Oral
Authors
Y. Kang*, Y. Jung*, W. Shin*, B. Kim*, S. Seo
Keywords
Synthetic Data Flood Segmentation Detection
Problem
Real flood imagery is scarce and lacks multi-task annotations (segmentation + depth + detection). Data collection during disasters is dangerous and costly.
Method
Generative pipeline combining diffusion-based image synthesis with automated multi-label annotation: segmentation masks, depth maps, and bounding boxes generated jointly.
Key Contributions
  • Large-scale synthetic flood dataset with 4 annotation types
  • Fully automated annotation pipeline — no manual labeling
  • Bridges sim-to-real gap for disaster response AI
Application
Disaster response, flood monitoring, emergency AI systems.
